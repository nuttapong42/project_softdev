Suvarnabhumi Station (Suvarnabhumi Station) is the terminal station in the high-speed rail line connecting 3 airports, the Suvarnabhumi Airport Railroad link. (Suvarnabhumi Airport Rail Link - SARL) which will be divided into High-speed rail linking 3 airports, which goes directly from Makkasan Station to Suvarnabhumi Airport, without stopping at each station, a distance of 25.7 kilometers, takes less than 15 minutes and the Suvarnabhumi Airport Rail Link City Line, which Make a stop along the way from Phaya Thai station to Suvarnabhumi And in the future will be able to connect to Don Mueang Airport and Bang Sue Central Station as well
